Sorting waste for a better world

We're dedicated to creating a better world through the sorting of waste into biodegradable and non-biodegradable materials. To help accomplish this, we've developed automated dustbins that open when a user approaches and tell them how full they are.

About the Company

GreenSort Solutions is a startup dedicated to creating a cleaner and more sustainable future. Our team is composed of experts in the fields of waste management, environmental engineering, and robotics. We believe that by encouraging people to sort their waste, we can make a significant impact on the health of our planet.
